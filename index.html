Single Family Homes across Cantons

Cut-Off Year: 2021

  • Central Switzerland: All these cantons have lower proportion of Single Family Homes this means below 50 % of the total number of buildings.
  • Eastern Switzerland and Lake Geneva:The proportion of Single Family Homes is in general over 50 %
  • Big Cities: The cantons Zurich (ZH) and Bern (BE) are obvious outliers in this plot due to their big cities (e.g., Zurich, Winterthur, Bern, Thun) and many inhabitants. cantons.

These observations underline some facts:

  • Cantons of Eastern Switzerland are known to be more rural and focused on agriculture and farming. Cities and villages are relatively small and less populated. This leads to the areas being less in demand and therefore, the price of land might be considerably more affordable.
  • Cantons like Zug (ZG) have a lower tax load so hounding tends to be more expensive (see more on that later).
