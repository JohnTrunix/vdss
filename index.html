Trends on the Swiss real estate market

Subheading Message 1

As can be seen from the chart, real estate prices in Switzerland have shown a continuous increase over the past 5 years, especially for new buildings and apartments. This indicates high demand and limited supply.

Development of real estate prices in Switzerland

Trends, demand and supply conditions, and regional differences

The analysis highlights current demand and supply conditions as well as the development of different property types to provide a comprehensive picture of the Swiss real estate market.

Influence of mortgage and construction loan rates

The chart shows the comparison between mortgage and construction loan interest rates and real estate prices in Switzerland. It can be seen that there is an inverse relationship between interest rates and real estate prices. In phases with low interest rates, real estate prices tend to rise, while they fall in phases with high interest rates.

Forecast of the price development

Forecast of the price development of real estate in Switzerland

The heat map shows the expected price development of real estate in various regions of Switzerland for the next 5 years, based on current data and taking into account mortgage and construction loan rates. The color markers highlight areas with expected price increases (red), stagnation (yellow) and weakening (green).
